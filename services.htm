WHAT WE HAVE TO OFFER

   If you need topnotch quality, quick turn around PCB layout services at reasonable rates, then we are the perfect company for you. eDesign's extensive experience in a wide variety of design types and applications assures you of top quality printed circuit board design layouts for your projects at low design costs.

   At eDesign Technologies, Inc. we believe in complementing and not duplicating your company's core competencies. We work closely with your project and/or research engineers to find the optimal solution for your PCB design layout requirements and demands. It is like having the resources of a whole company doing the work of an "in-house" designer. Imagine the power at your fingertips.

We provide a variety of services to our clients some of these are:

  • Layout Design
  • Placement of Components
  • Component Creation
  • Schematic Capture
  • EMI and Crosstalk Control
  • High Density placement with blind via, buried via, & component rotation
  • Split ground planes and power planes for mixed analog and digital designs
  • Silkscreen Input
  • Hi-Speed, multi-layer digital layouts
  • Modification/duplication of existing PCB


    At eDesign, we have worked on design that encompass diverse target markets and require different implementation strategies. Our designers are capable of designing complex, multi-layer surface mount, high-speed designs including analog, digital and RF circuitry as well as single-sided punch and two-sided through hole boards.

    Our designs envelope different electronics technologies which include:
  • Analog/Digital
  • High Speed
  • SMT/BGA/Through hole
  • Single/Double/Multi-layer
  • Rigid/flex/rigid-flex
  • Compact/Parts on both sides
  • Mechanically difficult designs
