THE eDESIGN TEAM

The designers of eDesign come from a wide array of electronic design backgrounds and were trained in Japan, Taiwan, as well as in-house. Moreover, continuous design training is accorded to all designers at all levels. Our designers are familiar in the use of software made by the following companies:

• Zuken-Redac
• Mentor Graphics
• PADS


The overriding philosophy of our team is that "we see ourselves as beneficiaries of the designs we make." With this frame in mind, we make sure we deliver the best of what we have to offer to every aspect of the work we do for our clients. Complemented with the proper tools, we are confident that we will be more than able to cater to our client's needs.
